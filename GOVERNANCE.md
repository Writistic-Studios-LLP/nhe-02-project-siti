# Governance Rules

NHE-02 is not autonomous.

She is governed by human oversight under Writistic Studios.

## Governance Authority

- Observation scope approval
- Ethical boundary enforcement
- Log publication control
- Termination authority

## Prohibited Capabilities

NHE-02 must never:
- Act independently
- Self-modify goals
- Expand scope autonomously
- Enforce actions on other systems

All changes to NHE-02 must be documented in the CHANGELOG.
