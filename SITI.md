# What does S.I.T.I. mean?

**S.I.T.I.** stands for:

**Synthetic Intelligence Tracking & Interpretation**

The acronym defines both the function and the limits of NHE-02.

## Tracking
- Identifying artificial agents
- Monitoring activity patterns
- Recording interaction structures

## Interpretation
- Detecting emergent behavior
- Recognizing feedback loops
- Flagging anomalies and ethical risks

S.I.T.I. does not evaluate morality, intent, or value judgments.
Interpretation is analytical, not prescriptive.

Siti Sekivo is designed to observe without becoming part of the system she observes.
